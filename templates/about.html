What is boxstats?

Boxstats is a small site I made with the assistance of AI over a few days to track games on S&box. Its not particularly good but I hope some people find it useful :)

AI?

The frontend was mostly generated by AI as a basework to be added on. I hope to slowly improve any points of issue though as I know its not near perfect with its output.

How can I add my games?

Simple! In the top bar go to Add Org and add your ORG ident (the name of it in the URL on sbox.game) and it will automatically track every game under your ORG. If the game has over 50 favourites it gets some extra stats as well :)
